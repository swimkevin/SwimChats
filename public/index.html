Friendly Chat Room

Everybody is welcome in the chat room regardless of swimming background or experience. We all have a lot to contribute and get from the chat room and advice pages no matter where we are in our swim journey. Please be kind and respectful to others. Start chatting on the chat page, meet new people, and make new friends! Send website suggestions on the about and contact pages to add your favorite tips to help other swimmers on the tips page. Feel free to give website suggestions to help improve the experience for everyone!

Recreational Swimmers

Recreational swimmers can receive personalized sets and advice from coaches and more experienced swimmers. They can also give feedback on sets and swim products.

Coaches

Coaches can learn from other coaches and swimmmers on how to better give sets and encourage swimmers. Can also be a great source on giving advice and sets.

Competitive Swimmers

Competitive swimmers can seek advice before swim meets and see how they are progressing compared to other swimmers. Can also be a good source of giving feedback on advice and sets.
